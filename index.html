Art works in Los Angeles

Introduction

In this project, our team plans to gather resources (style, location, ticket fee, etc.) of murals and art museums in Los Angeles and present the integrated information on a website. For people who are interested in both street art and traditional artworks, they are able to have easy access to get the information and experience both art works at one stop.
